Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my property on HomeHive?

You can register as a landlord and list your property through our dashboard. It typically takes 24-48 hours for approval.

Is there a fee to list properties?

Listing on HomeHive is free! We earn through premium features and partnerships.

How are reviews verified?

All reviews are from verified tenants who have completed their stay. We ensure authenticity and accuracy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets for secure transactions.

How can I report a property issue?

Use the "Report" button on any listing or contact our support team directly. We investigate all complaints.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and privacy practices to protect all user data.
